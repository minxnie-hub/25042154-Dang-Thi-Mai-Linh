# Decorative asset notes

The Japanese-study moodboard was researched using individual-object searches such as:

- `japanese omamori academic success graphic design isolated`
- `japanese sensu fan graphic design isolated`
- `hiragana flashcard graphic design isolated`
- `origami crane transparent SVG`
- `sakura petals isolated graphic design`
- `hanko stamp graphic design`

Pinterest was used only as visual moodboard research. The final decorations in `public/decor/` are original, individually-authored SVG objects, so the website does not embed a composite poster or hotlink third-party graphics.
